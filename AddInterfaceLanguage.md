# Add an interface language to your app

Go to the **Interface** section on the left side.

On the right click on the **Add Language…** button. 
Fill in the form:
![image|306x269](AddInterfaceLang.png) 
Click OK
On the top right there are three tabs, click on the **Translations** tab.
![image|288x96](clickTranslationsTab.png) 

Lower down there will be a tab for each language you want to use.
![image|610x292](blankTranslationStrings.png) 

Click on the **Tibetan** tab.
You need to fill in the Tibetan translations for each term.
